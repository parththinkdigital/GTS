Board 06 — Rules

Technical Brand Guidelines

Structural rules, spacing specifications, and usage principles for maintaining brand integrity.

✓ Do's

  • Use primary colors for key interfaces and CTAs
  • Maintain consistent typography hierarchy
  • Apply generous whitespace around the logo mark
  • Use high-quality imagery aligned with the brand
  • Ensure WCAG AA accessibility minimums
  • Keep brand consistency across all platforms

✗ Don'ts

  • Distort, stretch, or alter the logo proportions
  • Use unauthorized color variations outside the palette
  • Mix fonts from different families in the same view
  • Overuse bright gradients on critical text
  • Compromise readability for decorative effect
  • Apply low-contrast combinations against brand colors
